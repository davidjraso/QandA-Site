There are a number of clubs you can join at Wash U. Check out WU Churn if you have a hand for churning butter. Or if you have a fascination with beekeeping, Bee School is the club for you. There are a number of recreational sports clubs you can join like Rocklimbing and Kendo, or if you have a good palatte give Campus Kitchen a try. These are just a few of the many clubs that exist, so explore your options and try something new!

Across campus there are a number of different organizations. There are many cultural groups, like Ashoka, the Southeast Asian student group, the Association of Black Students, and the Asian American Association to name a few. There are also various religious organizations like Hillel and Catholic Student Union, and Pride Alliance is another great way to get involved in a great community. To take part in a national orgainzation, join Dance Marathon to have some fun and raise money for a great cause. There are so many other fantastic organizations you can join at Wash U.
